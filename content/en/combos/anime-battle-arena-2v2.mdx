<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step 2v2 Fight Plan">

Executing a coordinated strategy from the start of the match prevents your team from getting isolated. Use these steps to guide your team's positioning and decision-making during a competitive match.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Isolate a Single Target">
    At the start of the round, identify the weaker defensive target or the character with fewer escape tools. Use ranged poke or quick mobility skills to separate them from their teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait and Punish Dodges">
    Do not throw out your high-damage skills immediately. Approach with M1 strings to force the target to use their Q Dodge. Once their dodge is on cooldown, call it out to your teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Coordinate the Burst Window">
    With the target's dodge down, one player starts an M1 or aerial ladder combo. The second player positions themselves to catch the opponent with a special move or guard break as soon as the initial combo ends.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage the Off-Target Opponent">
    While bursting the primary target, one player must keep an eye on the second enemy. Use quick knockback skills or defensive blocks to prevent them from interrupting your teammate's combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Time Your Transformations">
    Once your G-meter is full, coordinate your awakenings. Do not transform at the same time unless you are pushing for a final game-winning stock. Staggering your transformations keeps the pressure active longer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Communication is Key">
Always call out when you are starting an M1 string or launching an opponent. This gives your teammate enough time to position their skills for a seamless follow-up attack.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Strategies and Counter-Play">

Survival in **anime battle arena 2v2** is just as important as landing high-damage combos. If you or your teammate dies early, winning a 1v2 situation is incredibly difficult against competent players. You must master defensive spacing, peel for your partner, and manage your defensive resources carefully.

| Defensive Scenario | Immediate Action | Tactical Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Teammate is caught in an M1 combo** | Hit the attacker with a fast, ranged skill or a quick knockback. | Interrupt the enemy combo and peel for your teammate. |
| **You are cornered by both opponents** | Hold F to block, space backward, and wait for your teammate to flank. | Minimize damage taken while your partner setup a counter-attack. |
| **Your guard is broken** | Call it out immediately and prepare to use Q Dodge the moment you recover. | Avoid follow-up burst damage from the second opponent. |
