<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Ranked Strategy">

Climbing the ranks requires a systematic approach to every single exchange. Follow this step-by-step combat loop to optimize your neutral game and secure more wins on the ladder.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maintain Spacing and Bait Mistakes">
    Never run directly at an opponent with a raw special move. Sprint using **Shift** to dance just outside their maximum M1 range, forcing them to miss an attack or waste their dodge (**Q**).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Confirm the M1 Starter">
    Once your opponent misses a move, close the distance and initiate your basic attack string (**Mouse1**). Confirming the first M1 hit ensures they are locked in hit-stun, making it safe to proceed with your combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Clean Combo Extensions">
    Combine your basic attacks with an aerial launcher (**Space + Mouse1**) to lift the opponent. While they are airborne, sequence your numerical special moves (1-4) to maximize damage before they hit the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage the Guard Break Game">
    If your opponent blocks with **F**, do not blindly hit their shield with basic attacks. Instead, bait their block and unleash a dedicated guard-breaking special move to stun them and initiate a full punish combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Deploy Awakening Strategically">
    Activate your transformation (**G**) when you have created sufficient space or knocked the opponent back. Never transform directly in front of an active opponent, as they can easily punish your activation animation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Combat Tip">
If you encounter an opponent who runs away constantly to stall for cooldowns, do not blindly chase them. Hold your ground, spam block (**F**) to negate their incoming long-range projectiles, and wait for them to overextend out of boredom.
</Callout>

</Section>
