<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Combo Mastery">

To succeed in the arena, you must move beyond "button mashing" and learn the rhythm of M1 strings. Combat in ABA is a dance where the **anime battle arena music** sets the pace. Follow these steps to build a reliable offensive foundation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Confirm the M1 String">
    Start by landing 3 to 4 Mouse1 hits. This creates hit-stun, locking the opponent in place and preventing them from jumping or attacking back immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute the Uptilt">
    After the third or fourth M1, press **Space + Mouse1**. This launches the opponent into the air, opening them up for an aerial combo or "ladder."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bridge with Special Moves">
    While the opponent is mid-air or recovering from the uptilt, use a special move (1-4) that has fast activation. For example, use a projectile or a dash-strike to maintain the combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset or Transform">
    If your Awakening bar is full, press **G** during a knockback to activate your theme. Use the invincibility frames of the transformation to reset the neutral game.
  </Step>
</Steps>
