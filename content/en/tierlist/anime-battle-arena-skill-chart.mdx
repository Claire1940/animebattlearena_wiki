Before attempting advanced combos on the **anime battle arena skill chart**, you must master the fundamental movement keys. Movement determines whether you can start an offensive string or escape an opponent's pressure.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Spacing with Sprint">
    Hold **Shift** to sprint. Use this to move in and out of your opponent's melee range to bait out missed attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Time Your Dodges">
    Press **Q** to dodge. Avoid spamming this key, as a wasted dodge leaves you vulnerable to a full punish string.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Your Guard">
    Hold **F** to block incoming basic attacks and non-guard-breaking skills. Always watch for the opponent's guard-break animations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Basic Combos">
    Use **Mouse1** for basic ground attacks, and combine it with **Space + Mouse1** to launch opponents into aerial combos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Defense Wins Matches">
Many beginner players lose rounds because they focus entirely on offensive skills. Learning to block with F and dodge with Q at the correct moments will instantly elevate your placement on the rank ladder.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combo Architecture & Skill Connections">

Connecting basic attacks into your character's special skills is the key to maximizing damage. A standard combo flow relies on hit-stun confirmation before committing to long cooldowns.

### Standard Combo Progression Flow

| Combo Phase | Action | Purpose | Input Tip |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Starter** | Ground M1 String | Creates initial hit-stun | Land 3 to 4 Mouse1 hits |
| **2. Launcher** | Space + Mouse1 | Sends opponent into the air | Press Space immediately after M1 |
| **3. Extension** | Aerial M1 String | Keeps opponent suspended | Follow up with rapid Mouse1 hits |
| **4. Finisher** | Skill Key (1-4) | Deals heavy burst damage | Activate a fast projectile or slam skill |

### Skill Usage Rules

- **Do not lead with skills**: Raw skills are easily blocked or dodged. Always confirm an M1 hit first.
- **Watch your cooldowns**: Using all four skills in a single exchange leaves you defenseless if the opponent survives.
- **Save your Awakening (G)**: Transformation can completely turn the tide of a match. Activate it when you have safely created distance or knocked the opponent back.

<Callout type="success" title="Awakening Strategy">
Do not activate your transformation while standing directly in front of an active opponent. Use a knockback skill or a successful dodge to create space before pressing G.
</Callout>
