<Section icon={Zap} title="Mastering Movement Across Different Terrains">

Navigating **anime battle arena maps** requires a deep understanding of the game's movement mechanics. The way you move determines whether you can initiate a combo or if you will be the one stuck in a block-stun.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Sprinting and Spacing">
    Use the **Shift** key to maintain a constant sprint. This allows you to stay just outside the opponent's M1 range. In larger maps, sprinting is vital for dodging long-range projectiles without wasting your Q dodge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Art of the Q Dodge">
    The **Q** key is your most valuable defensive tool. On maps with many obstacles, use your dodge to phase through small objects or to reposition behind an opponent who has just committed to a special move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Maneuvers">
    Combining **Space + Mouse1** allows for aerial attacks. Use this to launch opponents upward. On maps with elevation changes, aerial attacks can help you transition from low ground to high ground while maintaining offensive pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilizing Block (F)">
    When backed into a corner or a wall, hold **F** to block. While blocking, observe the opponent's rhythm. Most players will try to use a guard break move after three M1 hits. This is your cue to dodge out or counter-attack.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Input Command | Action | Map Strategy Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shift** | Sprint | Essential for crossing large arenas quickly |
| **Q** | Dodge | Use to escape corners or avoid AoE ultimates |
| **F** | Block | Best used when trapped against map boundaries |
| **Space + M1** | Aerial Attack | Use to extend combos away from ground hazards |
| **G** | Transform | Best used in the center of the map for maximum reach |

<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Positioning and Map Control">

In high-level Anime Battle Arena play, the player who controls the "center" of the map usually wins. Controlling the center gives you the most options for movement and prevents you from being easily cornered.

**Key Positioning Tactics:**
- **Center Control:** Stay near the middle to give yourself 360 degrees of escape options.
- **Wall Pinning:** If you land a hit, try to knock the opponent toward the nearest wall. This limits their ability to dodge sideways.
- **Baiting:** Stand near a hazard or edge to bait the opponent into a risky move, then use Q to dodge behind them and knock them into the danger zone.
- **Resource Management:** On large maps, keep track of where the health or energy pickups are (if applicable to the specific game mode) and maintain proximity to them.
