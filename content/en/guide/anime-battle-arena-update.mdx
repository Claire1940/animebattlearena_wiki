The recent updates also brought a wave of balance adjustments designed to tone down overperforming characters and make competitive matches fairer. 

| Character | Skill Adjusted | Specific Change | Balance Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vegito** | Savage Strike | Air version no longer procs if enemy is below Vegito | Prevent infinite air loops |
| **Afro Samurai** | Counter Cut | Added lingering i-frames on successful counter dash | Improve defensive utility |
| **Trafalgar Law** | Room | Cooldown increased, startup frames adjusted | Prevent spam in public matches |
| **Nanami** | Base 7:3 Combo | Hover mechanic added on down slam extensions | Prevent true infinite down slams |

**Key Balance Takeaways:**
- **Infinite Combo Prevention**: The developers are actively targeting "true infinite" combos, such as Nanami's down slam extensions and Vegito's air-to-ground loops, by adding hover states and vertical height checks.
- **Smarter Defensiveness**: Counter-focused characters like Afro Samurai have received lingering invincibility frames (i-frames) to reward players who successfully predict and react to enemy attacks.
- **Cooldown Increases**: High-tier zoning and domain-style moves have had their cooldowns increased to encourage active M1 physical engagement rather than passive running and skill-spamming.
