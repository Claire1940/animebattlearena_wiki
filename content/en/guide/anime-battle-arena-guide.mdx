<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Progression">

Executing clean combos is the key to winning matches consistently in Anime Battle Arena. Throwing out special moves raw will often result in you getting blocked and heavily punished. Follow this step-by-step training method to refine your execution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Confirm the M1 Starter">
    Begin your offensive string with basic Mouse1 attacks on the ground. Confirming that your first hit has connected ensures the opponent is in hit-stun before you execute subsequent inputs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Launch with Aerial Attacks">
    After landing 3 to 4 grounded M1 hits, press Space and Mouse1 simultaneously. This launches your opponent upward into an aerial state, leaving them unable to block.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Weave in Special Skills">
    While your opponent is suspended in the air, activate one of your character's fast special moves (Keys 1-4). This bridges the gap between basic attacks and high-damage finishes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Finisher">
    As the opponent begins to fall back to the ground, use your heavy skill or guard-breaking move to maximize the damage output of the exchange before they regain their footing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Practice Goal">
Head into a private server or training mode and practice landing this sequence 10 times in a row without dropping the combo. Consistency is far more valuable than trying to learn flashy, situational strings.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Ranked PvP Strategies & Matchup Tips">

Climbing the ranked ladder in 1v1 or 2v2 modes requires more than just knowing your combos. You must understand neutral spacing, cooldown management, and how to exploit your opponent's defensive habits.

| Strategy Focus | Key Objective | Tactical Execution |
|---|---|---|
| **Neutral Spacing** | Bait missed attacks | Stay just outside the enemy's M1 range using Shift to sprint back and forth |
| **Guard Breaking** | Punish passive blocking | Identify which of your character's skills bypasses F-block and use it when they hold block |
| **Awakening Management** | Turn the tide of battle | Press G only when you have created space or knocked the opponent away to prevent being interrupted |
| **2v2 Coordination** | Avoid isolation | Focus down the same target as your teammate while keeping your defensive cooldowns ready |

<Callout type="info" title="Matchup Knowledge">
Always pay attention to the opponent's character choice loading into the match. If they are playing a technical character like Aizen or Aoi Todo, expect them to rely heavily on timing-based counter-skills and teleports. Hold your block and play reactively.
</Callout>
