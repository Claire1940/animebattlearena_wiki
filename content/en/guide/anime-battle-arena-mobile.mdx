| Action | Mobile Input | PC Equivalent | Tactical Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double Tap Joystick / Run Button | Shift | Control spacing and chase down opponents |
| **Dodge** | Swipe / Q Button | Q | Escape combos or reposition during pressure |

| **Awakening** | G Button (When Full) | G | Enter transformation mode for enhanced stats |

Transitioning from casual play to ranked PvP on mobile requires dedicated practice. Use this step-by-step routine in a private server to optimize your touch accuracy and mechanical speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Up a Private Practice Space">
    Create or join an [Anime Battle Arena Private Server](https://www.roblox.com/games/1458767429/ABA) to practice without interruption. Private servers are the perfect environment to test touch button layouts, adjust camera sensitivity, and fight stationary or predictable bots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Spacing and Joystick Movement">
    Practice moving in and out of the bot's attack range using your virtual joystick. Double-tap to sprint, and practice circling the target while keeping your camera centered. Your goal is to bait out an attack without getting hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Drill the Defensive Block & Dodge">
    Hold your block button to absorb simulated attacks. Practice releasing block and instantly swiping or tapping your dodge button to slide behind the opponent. This movement is essential for escaping aggressive pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Clean M1-to-Skill Confirms">
    Approach the target and land three basic attacks. Immediately transition into one of your character's special skills (1-4). Repeat this drill until you can execute the transition seamlessly without any dropped inputs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Practice Goal">
Spend 10 to 15 minutes running this routine daily. Once you can land five consecutive air ladders on a moving target without dropping the combo, you are ready for public arenas.
