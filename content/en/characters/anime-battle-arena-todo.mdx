<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Base Moveset & Spatial Control">

To play anime battle arena todo effectively, you must master his dual-mode moves. Many of his abilities change properties depending on whether you are standing still or running. This mechanic makes him one of the most mechanically rewarding characters to pilot in close-quarters combat.

### Rubble Smash & Pebble Toss Mechanics
When running, **Rubble Smash** causes Todo to stomp the ground, creating a large rock that he punches forward. When standing still, he throws three pebbles onto the ground. 
- Both the rock and pebbles persist on the battlefield for up to 37 seconds.
- You can swap places with these objects using Boogie Woogie to escape danger or surprise enemies.
- Dropping pebbles with **Pebble Toss** allows you to aim your swap targets manually using your mouse.

### Black Flash & Boogie Kicks
Todo's dash kick can be enhanced into a **Black Flash** by pressing your M1 key exactly when the camera zoom effect ends. A successful Black Flash deals 25 damage and guardbreaks the opponent, while a missed timing deals only 15 damage and can be blocked. Meanwhile, **Boogie Kicks** acts as a slide-kick that flings opponents into the air while dropping a pebble at your starting position, setting up an immediate swap follow-up.

A unique feature of Todo's awakening is his mode duration. If you use **Boogie Woogie** while in your awakened state, your transformation timer will not decrease until the swap is either fully completed or cancelled. This allows skilled players to extend their high-damage state far beyond the standard duration.
