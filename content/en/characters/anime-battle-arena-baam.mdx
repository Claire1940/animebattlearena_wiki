<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide for Baam">

To excel with **anime battle arena baam**, you must master the "Ladder" technique. This involves launching the opponent into the air and following them up to continue the attack string. In 2026, the timing for these combos has become more precise due to engine updates, but the reward for execution remains the highest in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Approach the opponent with Shift-sprint and land 3 consecutive Mouse1 hits. Do not finish the fourth hit yet, as it will cause a knockback that ends the combo prematurely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Uptilt Launch">
    Jump and press Mouse1 simultaneously (Space + M1) to perform an "uptilt." This launches the enemy into the air, allowing you to follow them for an aerial string.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Skill Integration">
    While in the air, use the Black March (Skill 2) to dash through the opponent. This resets their fall gravity and allows you to land another set of aerial M1s.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Flare Wave Finisher">
    As you both return to the ground, immediately activate Flare Wave Explosion (Skill 3). If the opponent tries to block upon landing, this will break their guard and deal massive damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>
