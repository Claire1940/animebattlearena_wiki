<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The 7:3 Ratio Technique and Black Flash Passive">

Kento Nanami brings a unique rhythm-based mechanic to the **anime battle arena nanami** gameplay. Unlike standard characters who rely on simple cooldown management, Nanami players must master the 7:3 slider to maximize their effectiveness. This mechanic applies to **Bisecting Slash**, **Ratio Technique**, and **7:3 Combo**.

When you activate these moves, a bar with 10 ticks appears on your screen. A slider moves from left to right, slowing down specifically at the 7th tick (highlighted in red). Clicking exactly on this tick results in a **Critical Hit**, which adds secondary effects like Guardbreaks or increased damage.

| Mechanic | Requirement | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Hit** | Click on the 7th red tick | Adds Guardbreak/Extra Damage/Combo Extensions |
| **Black Flash** | Land 7 Critical Hits in a row | Massive damage buff, speed boost, lower cooldowns |
| **Sub-Mode Loss** | Miss a Black Flash slider | Immediate loss of all active buffs and mode ends |

<Callout type="success" title="Black Flash Synergy">
Landing 7 Critical Hits transforms your moves into Black Flash variants. In this state, your cooldowns are nearly halved, allowing for the only true infinite combo currently present in the game's competitive meta.
</Callout>

The risk-reward ratio for Nanami is steep. Missing a slider during a Black Flash chain doesn't just result in less damage; it resets your momentum entirely. Professional players often practice the slider timing in training modes to ensure they can maintain the Black Flash state throughout an entire stock.

</Section>
