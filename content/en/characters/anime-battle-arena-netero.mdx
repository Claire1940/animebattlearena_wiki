The core of **anime battle arena netero** gameplay lies in the interaction between his physical strikes and the Guanyin Boddhisattva. By default, his moves are fast and agile, but summoning the statue provides the range needed to control the neutral game.

### The Power of Ren and Ten
Netero's passive buffs are essential for maintaining pressure. Landing a ground **Kick** activates **Ren**, which provides a temporary 20% damage increase. Conversely, landing a **Punch** activates **Ten**, a defensive state that prevents opponents from breaking your guard. Mastering the rhythm of applying these buffs while maintaining a combo is what separates novice Netero players from experts.

### Stance-Modified Strikes
When the Boddhisattva is active, Netero gains access to "Hand" moves. These strikes have massive hitboxes:
- **First Hand**: A powerful overhead chop that functions as a guardbreak.
- **Second Hand**: Two consecutive slams that deal high damage (up to 52.8 when buffed) and provide knockback.
- **Third Hand**: A clasping motion that serves as a reliable combo starter or extender.

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanic Note">
The Boddhisattva moves share cooldowns with Netero's base martial arts. If you use Second Hand, you cannot immediately follow up with Dash Punch if they occupy the same slot in your rotation.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Awakening: The 100-Type Guanyin">

When Netero enters his Awakening state, he performs a prayer gesture, symbolizing his peak combat readiness. This state introduces devastating new abilities but also brings a unique, punishing health-based passive.

### The Cost of Combat: Limb Loss
Netero is one of the few characters in ABA whose performance actively degrades as he takes damage. This reflects his iconic battle against Meruem in the source material.

| HP Threshold | Physical Change | Gameplay Penalty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **50% HP** | Loses a Leg | Reduced movement speed, hopping animation |
| **25% HP** | Loses an Arm | M1 and move damage reduced by 50% |
| **Post-Zero Hand** | Drained Nen | Extremely slow, cannot run, emaciated state |

### Awakened Abilities
While the penalties are steep, the Awakened moveset is designed to end fights quickly before the thresholds are met.

### Trivia and Hidden Mechanics
- **The 67 Meme**: The move "Sixty-Seventh Hand" is a reference to a community meme; Netero never actually performed this specific numbered hand in the manga or anime.
- **The Mushroom Cloud**: The "Poor Man's Rose" mushroom cloud effect only appears the first time Netero dies/uses the ultimate in a match.
- **No-Stun State**: Successfully landing **Zero Hand** grants the user a "no-stun" period, allowing you to walk through attacks that would normally stagger you.
