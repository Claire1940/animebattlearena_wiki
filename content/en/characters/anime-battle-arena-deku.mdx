### The Power of Hyper Armor
One of Deku's defining traits is hyper armor. This mechanic allows him to continue his attack animation even if he takes damage. When fighting characters with fast, low-damage hits, Deku can simply "tank" the hit and deliver a much more powerful blow in return. This makes him exceptionally strong in "brawl" situations where both players are attacking simultaneously.
