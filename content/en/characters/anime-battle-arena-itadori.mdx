<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Base Kit Mechanics">

To maximize your efficiency with Yuji Itadori, you must master his two core mechanical pillars: the Black Flash timing and the Manji Block counter system.

### The Black Flash Timing
Black Flash is Yuji's high-skill ceiling tool. When activating the move, watch your camera closely. You must press your M1 key exactly as the camera zooms out. 

- **On Success**: You deal 25 damage, break the opponent's guard, and gain a massive M1 damage buff. This buff also adds Black Flash timings to your other active moves (Divergent Blow, Manji Kick, and Skull Smash), scaling their damage output significantly.
- **On Failure**: You deal a meager 10 damage, the move is blockable, and you miss out on the enhanced moveset potential.

### Countering with Manji Block
Manji Block is a unique defensive stance that takes approximately 0.5 seconds to become active. Because of this startup delay, it cannot be used on pure reaction to fast guardbreaks. Instead, use it predictively when you anticipate an opponent's aggressive wake-up option.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Stance Cooldown">

<Callout type="danger" title="The Trauma Penalty">
Sukuna is incredibly powerful, but he comes with a severe drawback. If you kill at least one opponent during your awakening, Yuji will regain control, set your HP to 60, and enter a 15-second **Trauma state** where he falls to his knees. If you survive these 15 seconds, you regain 60 HP. However, if you kill **nobody** during the awakening, you exit the mode safely without entering the Trauma state.
</Callout>
