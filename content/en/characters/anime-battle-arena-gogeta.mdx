<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Super Saiyan & Super Saiyan Blue Transformations">

Gogeta features a unique two-stage awakening system. He first transforms into Super Saiyan (SSJ), enhancing his base moves. Once you deal at least **120 damage** in SSJ, you can activate **The Strongest Fusion** to transform into Super Saiyan Blue (SSB), which heals you for 50 HP and completely replaces your move set.

### Super Saiyan (SSJ) Move Set

| **Super Mad Dance** | 20.0 | 18s | Counter, Ignores Block, Slam Down |
| **Stardust Fall** | 2.0 / hit | 15s | Guardbreak, Teleports on Guardbreak |

| Move Name | Damage (Normal / Max) | Cooldown | Special Mechanic |
|---|---|---|---|
