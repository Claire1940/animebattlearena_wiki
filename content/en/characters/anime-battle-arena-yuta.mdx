<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Strategy">

To excel with **anime battle arena yuta**, you must move beyond random skill usage. Effective combos in ABA rely on the "M1 -> Skill -> M1" loop. Yuta's ability to stun makes these loops much easier to execute than other characters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Neutral Opener">
    Start by fishing for an M1 connect or a successful dodge. Use your **Shift** key to sprint around the opponent, staying just outside their M1 range. Wait for them to miss a move, then dash in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Cursed Speech Confirm">
    Once you land a 3-hit M1 string, immediately press **1 (Cursed Speech)**. This will "crumple" the opponent, leaving them defenseless for several seconds. Do not finish the 4th M1 hit as it causes knockback.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Extension">
    While the opponent is crumpled, perform an **Uptilt (Space + M1)**. While in the air, follow up with 3 aerial M1s and then use **2 (Rika Strike)** to slam them back into the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    As they land, use **3 (Katana Flurry)** to catch their get-up animation. This ensures maximum damage before they can regain their block or dodge cooldown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Dodge Management">
Avoid using your **Q (Dodge)** offensively. Yuta needs his dodge to escape "ladders" (aerial combos) from high-mobility characters like Minato or Killua. Save it for defensive resets.
</Callout>

</Section>
