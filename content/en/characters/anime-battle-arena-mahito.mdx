<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Mahito Combo Guide">

To succeed with **anime battle arena mahito**, you must learn how to chain his physical strikes with his transfigured summons. His combo structure relies heavily on confirmed M1 strings and downslam setups.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Confirm the M1 Starter">
    Begin your offense with a standard 4-hit M1 string to lock the opponent in hit-stun. Do not finish the knockback hit; instead, prepare to transition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Launch with Body Repel">
    Immediately follow your M1s with **Body Repel**. This launches three transfigured worms that force a guardbreak if blocked, keeping your opponent vulnerable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Extend via Self-Transfiguration">
    Use **Self-Transfiguration** to spin your arm-blades. Hold the jump button during this move to mutate into a large spike ball, crushing the enemy and dragging them forward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Idle Transfiguration">
    Finish the sequence by closing the distance and activating **Idle Transfiguration**. This grab deals massive unblockable damage to seal the exchange.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Wasted Cooldowns">
Never throw out **Idle Transfiguration** raw in neutral. It has a short range of 4.6 studs and is highly punishable if it misses. Always confirm a hit first.
</Callout>

</Section>
