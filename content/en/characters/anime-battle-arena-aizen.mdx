To maximize your efficiency with **anime battle arena aizen**, you must practice specific combo strings in training mode before heading into ranked 1v1s. Aizen's combos are highly rewarding but require precise timing to prevent the opponent from "dropping" out of the sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Start by approaching the opponent with **Shift (Sprint)**. Land three M1 hits. Do not finish the fourth hit, as the knockback will push them too far away for a follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Skill Integration">
    Immediately after the third M1 hit, activate **Skill 1 (Raikōhō)**. This provides immediate damage and keeps the opponent staggered.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Ladder Technique">
    Use **Space + Mouse1** to launch the opponent into the air. While they are airborne, land two more M1s. This is known as "laddering" and is essential for high-level ABA play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    As the opponent begins to fall, use **Skill 2 (Kurohitsugi)**. If timed correctly, the coffin will spawn exactly where they land, trapping them for a massive burst of damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Combo Tip">
If your opponent is playing defensively, use your **Q (Dodge)** to dash behind them and immediately start your M1 string. Aizen's **Shunpo (Skill 3)** can also be used mid-combo to reset your positioning if you miss a hit.
