<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Form Moveset Analysis">

Jin Woo's moveset evolves significantly as he levels up. Early forms focus on individual dagger strikes, while later forms introduce the "Ruler's Authority" and "Monarch's Domain" which provide massive utility.

### Early Game: E-Rank & Underdog
In these stages, you must rely on **Dodge Roll** to survive. **Hunter's Desperation** is your primary combo starter, lunging forward with a guard break. Once you hit Underdog, you gain **Assassin's Step**, allowing you to disappear and reappear behind opponents for a slam.

### Mid Game: Necromancer & Ant Killer
This is where Jin Woo becomes a projectile threat. **Ruler's Hand** allows you to throw rocks with hyper armor, while **Summon: Tank** and **Summon: Beru** provide massive hitboxes that can start or extend combos.

### Late Game: Giant Killer & King
**Monarch's Domain** is the highlight here, creating a field that grants Jin Woo a 15% damage boost, 15% damage resistance, and a speed boost. **Ruler's Authority** launches multiple daggers that guard break, making it nearly impossible for enemies to stay defensive.

- **Endless Eclipse**: A powerful vortex that drags in all nearby enemies before exploding for 22 damage. It has hyper armor, making it a perfect "get off me" tool.
