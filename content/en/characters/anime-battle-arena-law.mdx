<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Moveset: Outside and Inside Room">

Law features two distinct movesets depending on whether he is standing inside his Room. Managing these two states is key to high-level play.

### Moves Outside of Room

When fighting without his domain active, Law relies on fast, direct physical strikes to open up opponents or defend himself.

| Move Name | Damage | Cooldown | Properties & Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slash** | 20 | 14s | Guardbreak, Combo Starter, Melee |
| **Counter Shock** | 22 | 8s | Guardbreak, Knockback, Projectile |
| **Room** | N/A | 5s | Moveset Modifier, 12-stud initial size |

### Moves Inside Room

Once **Room** is active, Law's standard skills transform into powerful spatial manipulation abilities.

| Move Name | Damage | Cooldown | Properties & Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shambles** | N/A | 7s | Teleportation, Swap places, Traversal |
| **Amputate** | 28 | 33s | Multi-Hit, Guardbreak (2nd Hit), Combo Extender |
| **Amputate Smash** | 63 (Total) | 33s | Dual target slam, Ignores Block |
| **Takt** | 21-42 | 3s/10s | Summon/Throw rocks, Slam Down, I-frames on summon |
| **Gamma Knife** | 32 | 16s | Guardbreak, Knockback, Combo Finisher |
