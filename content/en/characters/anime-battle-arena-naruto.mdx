<Section icon={Swords} title="Starter Characters: Kid Naruto & Sasuke Movesets">

The foundation of the **anime battle arena naruto** experience lies in the "Baby" versions of the main protagonists. These characters are designed to teach you the basics of M1 combos and guard breaking.

### Kid Naruto
Kid Naruto is a rushdown specialist. His primary tool is the **Rasengan**, which is a reliable guard break that sends opponents flying. This is essential for maps with ring-out potential like the Tournament of Power. His **Uzumaki Barrage** and **Shadow Clones** are great for extending combos, but be careful—they can be blocked.

### Kid Sasuke
Kid Sasuke offers more versatility with his **Fireball Jutsu** and **Shurikens**. Unlike Naruto, Sasuke's **Chidori** does not have as much knockback but serves as his primary guard break. His Awakening, the **Curse Mark**, gives him access to the **Fuma Shuriken** and a homing Chidori, making him a nightmare for players who like to keep their distance.

| Move Name | Character | Blockable? | Damage Type |

| Uzumaki Barrage | Kid Naruto | Yes | Combo Multi-hit |
| Shadow Clone | Kid Naruto | Yes | Summon Attack |
| Chidori | Kid Sasuke | **No** | Piercing |
| Fireball Jutsu | Kid Sasuke | Yes | Burn DOT |
| Lion's Barrage | Kid Sasuke | Yes | Vertical Launch |

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Clones">
Shadow Clones in the base form only perform a single click attack. Do not rely on them to win a trade; use them as a distraction to land your Rasengan.
</Callout>

### Zabuza: The Demon of the Mist
Zabuza is unique due to his **Hiding in the Mist** ability. It fogs the enemy's screen and makes Zabuza invisible. 
- **Decapitate**: A shoulder bash followed by a sword slam. **Cannot be blocked.**
- **Sword Throw**: If you throw your sword, your M1 clicks actually become faster, but you lose range until you pick the sword back up.

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Strategy">

<Section icon={Shield} title="The 2026 Meta: Itachi and Pain Strategies">

In the current **anime battle arena naruto** meta, Itachi and Pain are the gold standard for high-level play. Their kits are designed to punish mistakes and control the flow of the match.

### Itachi Uchiha
Itachi's base form relies on **Crow Genjutsu** for survivability. If activated, any damage taken will cause Itachi to disperse into crows and return at full health. His **Fireball** is notably unblockable, dealing a flat 20 damage.
- **Awakening**: Becomes "Criminal Itachi" with the Susanoo.
- **Yata Mirror**: Grants temporary invincibility.
- **Amaterasu**: Unblockable black flames that drain health but cost Itachi some HP.

### Pain (Six Paths)
Pain is arguably the most annoying character to fight due to **Almighty Push**. 
- **Universal Pull**: Drags the enemy in for a slam.
- **Almighty Push**: A massive AOE that cannot be blocked.
- **Awakening**: Transformation into the "Deceased" state. His range increases significantly, and **Planetary Devastation** creates a meteor that pulls in and destroys everyone in the vicinity.

| Ability | Character | Meta Impact | Best Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tsukuyomi | Itachi | High (Stun) | Don't look (Distance) |
| Almighty Push | Pain | Extreme (AOE) | Run away during charge |
| Susanoo | Itachi | High (Defense) | Wait for meter drain |
| Planetary Devastation | Pain | Game Ending | Kill Pain before launch |

<Callout type="success" title="Winning Tactic">
As Itachi, use your Crow Genjutsu right before an opponent uses their ultimate. You will negate their entire meter while resetting your own health.
</Callout>

### Madara Uchiha (Reanimated)
Madara is a beast in both base and awakened forms. His **Great Fireball** involves a backflip that makes him hard to hit, and it is completely unblockable.
- **Shattered Heaven**: In his Rinnegan awakening, he can drop two meteors.
- **Wood Style**: Summons dragons that automatically track and attack the nearest player.
