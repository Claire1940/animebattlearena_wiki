<Section icon={Map} title="Anime Battle Arena Boa Hancock Overview">

Boa Hancock brings a highly aggressive, utility-focused playstyle to the One Piece roster in Anime Battle Arena (ABA). Operating as a mid-tier to high-tier threat depending on your ability to land skill shots, her entire kit revolves around the **Petrification** passive. By utilizing charm indicators and converting them into full stuns, she can lock down mobile opponents and execute devastating, high-damage combos.

She is also unique for her basic attack animations. Boa Hancock is the fourth character in the game to utilize the Black Leg M1 style, sharing this distinction with Post-Timeskip Sanji, Pre-Timeskip Sanji, and Kizaru.

<Callout type="tip" title="Passive Mechanic: Petrification">
Hitting enemies with Pistol Kiss, Dazzling Kick, Mero Mero Mellow, Kiss Rifle, Perfume Femur: Magna (dropkick), or Perfume Breaker (last hit) applies a **Charm** status (indicated by floating hearts) for 10 seconds. Hitting a charmed enemy with any of these moves again turns them into stone for 4 seconds. M1 attacks or skills (except Kiss Rifle) deal **6 bonus damage** to petrified targets and break the stone, knocking them back.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Form Move List and Properties">

In her base form, Boa Hancock relies on a mix of blockable combo starters and unblockable guardbreaks to set up her petrification loops. Understanding the exact properties of each move is crucial for consistent performance in ranked matches.

| Move | Type | Damage (Base / Petrified) | Properties |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pistol Kiss** | Projectile | 12 / 18 | Blockable, Combo Starter, Charms |
| **Dazzling Kick** | Melee | 16 / 22 | Guardbreak, Knockback, Combo Starter, Charms |
| **Mero Mero Mellow** | Projectile | 21 (Multi-hit) | Guardbreak, Multi-hit, Combo Extender, Charms |
| **Perfume Femur** | Melee | 19 / 25 | Blockable, Multi-hit, Slam Down, Combo Extender |

### Base Move Breakdown

- **Pistol Kiss**: Boa blows a kiss, firing a heart-shaped projectile. This serves as an excellent long-range poke and combo starter to apply the initial charm.
- **Dazzling Kick**: A forward-leaping strike. Because it guardbreaks, it is highly reliable for catching defensive players off guard and forcing a charm status.
- **Mero Mero Mellow**: A wide, heart-shaped beam that multi-hits and pushes enemies back. This move is a fantastic combo finisher or extension tool when paired with Perfume Femur.
- **Perfume Femur**: A sweeping kick followed by a rapid kick barrage. Note that the final downslam effect only activates if the initial barrage makes contact with an opponent.

<Callout type="warning" title="Combo Execution Warning">
If you do not hit anything during the initial sweep of **Perfume Femur**, the downslam animation will fail to trigger, leaving you highly vulnerable to counter-attacks.
</Callout>
