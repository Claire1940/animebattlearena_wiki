<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Kit: Moon Breathing and Core Moves">

Kokushibo's base kit is centered around his **Moon Breathing** passive. This passive allows him to buff his sword, increasing the effectiveness of his subsequent attacks. While the visual effect on the sword lasts approximately 7 seconds, the mechanical benefits can be sustained through aggressive play. Understanding the difference between standard moves and their **Crescent Moon Variants** is the key to mastering this character.

| Move Name | Type | Damage (Standard) | Damage (Variant) | Property |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **First Move** | Projectile | 6.5 (Multi-hit) | 21.5 | Blockable / Combo Extender |
| **Second Move** | Melee | 15 (Total) | 20-30 | Guardbreak (Variant dependent) |
| **Third Move** | Melee | 7 | 20 | Blockable / Knockback |
| **Fourth Move** | Melee | 7 | 17 | Guardbreak / Hyper Armor |

The second move is particularly unique because it offers two different guardbreak slashes. The first slash deals 5 damage, while the second deals 10 and provides a knockback effect. Choosing the right slash depends on whether you want to continue a combo or reset the neutral game.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Passive Management">
Even if the visual purple aura disappears from Kokushibo's sword, the Moon Breathing buff may still be active. Always attempt your variant moves if you have recently activated the passive.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the 120-0 Combo">

To truly dominate with Kokushibo in **Anime Battle Arena**, you must learn his 120-0 combo. This sequence is designed to take an opponent from full health to zero in a single exchange. The combo relies on precise M1 timing and the use of "uptilts" and "downslams" to keep the enemy in a hit-stun state.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opener">
    Start with 4 M1 slashes, followed immediately by an **uptilt** and a **downslam**. This creates the initial opening for your variants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Pearl Flower Bridge">
    Activate the **2nd Form: Pearl Flower Moongazing** (Crescent Moon Variant). Follow this with 3 more M1s to maintain the stun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Combo Extension">
    Perform another 4 M1s, uptilt, and downslam sequence. Then, trigger the **16th Form: Moonbow, Half Moon** variant for massive damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    End the sequence with 4 M1s, an uptilt, a downslam, and the **5th Form: Moon Spirit Calamitous Eddy**. If executed correctly, the opponent will be defeated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Combo Consistency">
Practice the timing of the Crescent Moon variants in the training room. If you are too slow, the enemy will be able to use their Evasive and escape the 120-0 loop.
</Callout>
