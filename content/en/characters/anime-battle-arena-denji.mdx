### Hybrid Form and the Fire Passive
When the blood bar is completely full, Denji ignites himself. This "Fire Mode" adds burn damage to his M1 strings and his primary moves, **Blood Frenzy** and **Grind**. However, this power comes at a cost, as he slowly loses health while the flames are active.
