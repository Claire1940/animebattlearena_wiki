### Standard Base Combo
This sequence is the bread and butter for Toji players, maximizing damage before the opponent can recover their block.

1. **4 M1s** into an **Uptilt**.
2. Throw a **Knife** to catch them mid-air.
3. Land another **4 M1s** followed by a **Downslam**.
4. Use **Worm** immediately to extend the hitstun.
5. Follow up with **Sudden Attack** to bypass any panic blocks.
6. Finish with **Jump** into a final **Throw**.
