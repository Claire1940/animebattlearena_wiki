<Section icon={Swords} title="Yoruichi Shihōin Base Moveset Analysis">

Yoruichi Shihōin, the former captain of the 2nd Division, brings an unparalleled level of speed to the **anime battle arena yoruichi** experience. Unlike many other characters who rely on heavy projectiles, Yoruichi is a pure melee specialist. Her base kit is designed to punish mistakes and keep the pressure high through constant movement.

| Move Name | Damage | Cooldown | Properties |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flash Combo** | 29 | 12s | Blockable, Multi-Hit, Combo Starter |
| **Down Smash** | 25 | 18s | **Guardbreak**, Slam Down, Extender |
| **Pounce** | 20 | 15s | Blockable, **Auto-Aim**, Knockback |
| **Flip** | 15 | 20s | **Ignores Block**, I-Frames, Grab |

**Flash Combo** serves as her primary engagement tool. By using flashstep to instantly close the distance, she can catch opponents off-guard. However, because it is blockable, seasoned players will wait for the animation to end before counter-attacking. This is where **Down Smash** becomes vital; it functions as a powerful guardbreak that forces the opponent to stay mobile rather than hiding behind a block.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Maneuver">
You can use **Down Smash** while in the air to perform a diving punch. This is excellent for catching players who are jumping to avoid ground-based attacks.
</Callout>

**Pounce** is unique because of its auto-aim property. While it deals moderate damage and provides knockback, it cannot be used in the air, limiting its utility to ground skirmishes. Finally, **Flip** is perhaps her most technical move. It ignores blocks entirely and provides invincibility frames (I-frames), allowing Yoruichi to "phase" through certain attacks if timed correctly.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Shunkō Awakening: The Goddess of Flash">

When Yoruichi activates her **Shunkō** transformation, she enters her most dangerous state. Lightning engulfs her body, tearing her clothing and granting her enhanced mobility. In this form, her dashes have virtually no cooldown, making her nearly impossible to pin down in the **anime battle arena yoruichi** meta.

| Awakening Move | Damage | Cooldown | Properties |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flash Break** | 30 | 12s | **Guardbreak**, Combo Starter |
| **Shunko Barrage** | 35 | 15s | Blockable, Multi-Hit, AOE |
| **Raijin Senkei** | 35 | 20s | **Guardbreak**, Projectile, AOE |
| **rawr (Ultimate)** | 42 | 45s | **Ignores Block**, I-Frames, Finisher |

The awakening moveset shifts from pure melee to include powerful area-of-effect (AOE) attacks. **Raijin Senkei** creates massive columns of electrical energy, providing Yoruichi with a much-needed long-range guardbreak. This allows her to pressure opponents even when they attempt to keep their distance.

<Callout type="success" title="Ultimate Strategy">
The move **rawr** (Shunkō: Raiju Senkei: Shunryū Kokubyō Senkei) is her ultimate finisher. It teleports her to the opponent and delivers a massive barrage of hits. Since it ignores blocks and provides I-frames, use it when the opponent is at low health to guarantee a stock.
</Callout>

**Shunko Barrage** is an excellent tool for clearing groups in team fights or finishing a combo with high AOE damage. The lightning shockwaves generated by this move make it difficult for nearby enemies to intervene, providing Yoruichi with a safe window to execute her full damage rotation.

</Section>
