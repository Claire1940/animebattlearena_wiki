<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Plasma Storm Transformation (Awakening)">

When Accelerator fills his awakening bar, he unlocks **Plasma Storm**, transforming into an absolute powerhouse with reduced cooldowns, massive damage boosts, and a lethal ultimate move.

### Plasma Storm Moveset

| Move Name | Cooldown | Damage | Key Properties |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accelerate** | 9 Sec | 25 | Runs behind target, shoves them forward |
| **Quake** | 9 Sec | 40 | High-damage seismic explosion, upflings |
| **Plasma** | One-time | 90 | Bypasses Guard, massive range (24 studs) |
| **Redirection** | 10 Sec | Variable | Lasts 4.2 seconds, highly defensive |

### Awakening Features & Trivia
- **Damaging Awakening**: Accelerator is the first character in ABA to have an awakening animation that deals damage (40 damage and grants 144 points).
- **Extended Counter**: Redirection's base active duration increases to 4.2 seconds during Plasma Storm.
- **One-Shot Potential**: Combining a basic combo string into **Plasma** can instantly deplete an opponent's life bar.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide">

To maximize your damage output with the **anime battle arena accelerator**, you must master his transition between light attacks (M1s) and his special moves.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Bread & Butter Ground Combo">
    Execute 4 basic M1 attacks, follow up with an Upfling, and immediately cast **Grasp** to trap them in the pillars.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Aerial Extension">
    While the opponent is trapped in the pillars, execute **Accelerate (Air)** to fly towards them, punch them into the pillars, and deal burst damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Ground Follow-up">
    Land safely, execute 3 M1 attacks, perform an Uptilt followed by a Downslam, and finish with **Quake (Air)** to slam them back into the dirt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Awakening One-Shot Setup">
    During Plasma Storm, perform 4 M1s, launch them with an Upfling, follow with **Quake (Air)**, chase with **Accelerate**, land 5 M1s, and immediately fire **Plasma** to secure the stock.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Combo Optimization">
If the opponent blocks your **Grasp (Ground)**, it leaves them in a state where they can block subsequent non-guardbreaking moves. Coordinate with teammates in 2v2s to follow up with guardbreaks.
</Callout>
