An **anime battle arena ban** can instantly halt your progress, lock away your expensive gamepasses, and sever your connection to the community. In Anime Battle Arena (ABA), bans generally fall into two categories: competitive character bans during Ranked draft phases, and permanent account bans issued by the game's moderation team.

Account bans are much more severe than draft bans. These restrictions are tied to your Roblox User ID or your local IP address. If you receive an account ban, you will see a simple notification stating that you have been banned when attempting to join the game.

| Ban Type | Duration | Primary Trigger | Resolution Method |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Character Ban** | 1 Match | Ranked Draft Phase | Select a backup character |
| **Account Ban** | Permanent | Exploiting, Cheating, Glitching | Official Discord Appeal |
| **IP Ban** | Permanent | Multi-accounting, Shared device cheating | Network-wide appeal process |

<Callout type="warning" title="Shared Device Warning">
If you share a PC or internet connection with another Roblox player who exploits, your account can suffer a collateral IP ban. The moderation system cannot easily distinguish between different individuals on the same network.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Appeal an Account Ban">

If you believe your account restriction was issued in error, you must follow the official channels to contact the moderation team. Direct messaging staff members on Discord is highly discouraged and often ignored due to the volume of requests they receive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Official Discord Server">
    Access the official Anime Battle Arena Discord server. This is the primary hub where all community moderation takes place.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Appeal Channel or Ticket System">
    Navigate to the designated support or appeal channels. Do not ping administrators or developers directly in public chat rooms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify the Correct Moderators">
    Focus your appeal efforts on the active in-game moderators. While community administrators handle Discord-specific issues, specialized in-game moderators handle Roblox account restorations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Submit Your Case Honestly">
    Provide your exact Roblox username, explain your situation without hostility, and present any evidence that proves your innocence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Moderator Roles">
In-game moderation staff like K1 and Strict possess the authority to lift in-game bans. Community leaders like Hasheem primarily focus on Discord community management rather than individual Roblox account restorations.
</Callout>

</Section>
