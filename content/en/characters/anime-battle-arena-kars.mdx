<Callout type="warning" title="Important Mechanic">
When using **Ripple Fisticuffs**, mini volcanoes will spawn under nearby players, spewing lava for passive damage. However, if your initial punch guardbreaks the target, the volcanoes **will not spawn**. Plan your spacing carefully to maximize passive stage hazard damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide for Kars">

To play **anime battle arena Kars** at a high level, you must memorize his combo routes. His ability to transition from basic M1 attacks into auto-aim teleports makes his combo strings highly reliable even in high-ping environments.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Base Form Ground Starter">
    Begin with **4 basic M1 attacks**, then perform an **upfling** to launch the opponent into the air.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Aerial Teleport Extension">
    Immediately activate **Victory** to teleport above the airborne opponent, slamming them back down to the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ground Follow-up">
    Run up to the fallen opponent and land another **4 M1 attacks**, then immediately execute **Beautiful Violence** to steer them and lock them in a prismatic slash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combo Finisher">
    Finish the sequence with another **4 M1s**, an **uptilt**, a **kickback**, and immediately cast **Brilliant Bone Blades** for a high-damage guardbreak finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Awakening Combo Route">
In Ultimate Life Form, use this sequence for massive damage: **4 M1s** -> **Upfling** -> **Predator Wings** -> **Supreme Genes** -> **4 M1s** -> **This cannot be!** (Deals over 100 total damage).
</Callout>
