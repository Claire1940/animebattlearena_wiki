<Section icon={Shield} title="Base Form: Tactical Espionage Action">

In his base form, **anime battle arena solid snake** relies on utility and stealth to control the neutral game. His kit is designed to punish over-aggressive players who ignore their surroundings.

**The Mk22 Tranquilizer**
The Mk22 is Snake's bread-and-butter projectile. You have 7 rounds before a 10-second reload cooldown. Landing 4 rounds on a single target causes them to "crumple" into a sleep state for 10 seconds. This is a free opening for any combo or a reset.

**Cardboard Box & Chaff Grenade**
The Box provides invisibility as long as you stay out of the enemy's yellow vision cone. More importantly, it acts as a "substitute," tanking one free hit without Snake taking damage. The Chaff Grenade is a versatile tool that breaks guards and creates a field where enemy cooldowns are frozen.

| Move | Primary Use | Special Property |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mk22** | Combo Extender / CC | 4 shots = 10s Sleep |
| **Box** | Defensive / Stealth | Tanks 1 free hit |
| **Chaff Grenade** | Guard Break / Utility | Freezes enemy cooldowns |
| **Hold Up** | Damage Buff | Doubles next Mk22/M1 damage |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
The Box freezes your own cooldowns while active. Do not stay inside it for too long if you are waiting for your Mk22 or Chaff Grenade to return. Use it briefly to reposition or dodge a lethal ultimate.
</Callout>

**Mastering the Hold Up**
The Hold Up is perhaps the most difficult move to land but offers the highest reward. You must be directly behind the opponent. If successful, they are stunned for 5 seconds. Your next M1 or Mk22 shot will deal double damage. In 2026 meta, players often use the Chaff Grenade's stun to loop behind an opponent for an easy Hold Up.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Alert Phase: Lethal Force Authorized">

When Snake activates his awakening, he enters the **Alert Phase**. The transition begins with a Codec Call, a brief animation where Snake kneels. Once the alarm blares and the screen flashes red, Snake switches to a lethal arsenal designed for rapid elimination.

**The M4 & Stealth Camo**
The M4 replaces the Mk22, firing a rapid burst of lethal rounds. While it has some spread, it can easily shred half an opponent's health bar if landed at close-to-mid range. Stealth Camo is a direct upgrade to the Box, providing total invisibility without the vision cone restriction. If used while being hit, it functions as an evasive backflip.

**Grenade & Chokehold**
The High-Explosive Grenade features a parabolic arc and a massive guard-breaking explosion. It can bounce off walls, making it unpredictable. The Chokehold is a command grab that ignores guards. By tapping the button, you can strangle the opponent up to 12 times, leading to a 2-second knockdown stun.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Mastery">

To dominate with **anime battle arena solid snake**, you must master his specific combo timings. Because he only has 3 M1s, his extenders are crucial for 100-to-0 sequences.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Basic Bread and Butter">
    Start with 1 M1, followed by an Up-tilt. While the opponent is in the air, fire one Mk22 round. As they fall, perform a Down-slam. This sets up the perfect distance for a follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Hold Up Loop">
    Immediately after the Down-slam from Step 1, use Hold Up. Since the opponent is recovering, you can easily get behind them. Follow this with 3 M1s and another Mk22 round to maximize the double damage buff.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sleep Finisher">
    If the opponent has already taken two Mk22 hits earlier in the match, this combo will trigger the Sleep state. Once they are asleep, wait for your cooldowns or land a full M1 string into a Chaff Grenade for a guaranteed guard break upon their awakening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Awakened Chokehold Chain">
    In Alert Phase, land 2 M1s, then immediately use Chokehold. Spam the button for the full 12 strangles. As they fall, point-blank them with the M4 burst. This sequence is almost impossible to escape without an evasive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Combo Efficiency">
Snake's "Combo 1" (M1 + Uptilt + Mk22 + Downslam + Hold Up + 3 M1s + Mk22) deals approximately 39.8 damage. In the 2026 balance, this means an opponent only needs one more full M1 string to be within kill range.
