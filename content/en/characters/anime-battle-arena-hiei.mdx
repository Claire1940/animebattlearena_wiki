<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Moveset and Tactical Analysis">

Hiei's base form is already dangerous due to his mobility. He possesses two primary guardbreaks in his base kit, which is rare for non-awakened characters. This allows him to initiate combos against players who rely too heavily on the block button.

| Move Name | Damage | Cooldown | Primary Property |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Afterimage Strikes** | 33 (7x3 + 12) | 12s | Multi-hit, Knockback, Combo Finisher |
| **Blender** | 20 | 20s | **Guardbreak**, Aggressive, Knockback |
| **Meteor** | 20 | 20s | **Guardbreak**, Slam Down, Combo Extender |
| **Behind You** | Variable | Variable | Teleport, Multi-slash, Cancellable |

### Move Breakdown

1.  **Afterimage Strikes**: This is your primary damage dealer and finisher. It creates multiple versions of Hiei to strike the opponent. It is blockable, so ensure the enemy's guard is broken before using it.
2.  **Blender**: A circular sword swing. Because it guardbreaks, it is excellent for catching players off-guard during a neutral exchange.
3.  **Meteor**: Hiei dives from the air. This move is a "Slam Down," meaning it can be used to ground an opponent and immediately follow up with another attack.
4.  **Behind You**: The core of Hiei's technical play. You can cancel this move into other abilities to bait out a block or use it after a downslam to deal increased damage without the teleport.

<Callout type="info" title="Technical Note">
**Meteor** can be blocked by physical objects in the environment, such as Kakashi's Mud Wall. Always ensure you have a clear line of sight to the ground when activating this move.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Jagan Eye Awakening: Unleashing the Dragon">

When Hiei activates his **Jagan Eye (Transformation)**, he becomes arguably the most oppressive character in **anime battle arena**. His Awakening kit features four different guardbreaks, meaning an opponent practically cannot block any of his special moves.

| Awakening Move | Damage | Cooldown | Special Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mortal Flame Fist** | 40 | 17s | **Guardbreak**, Auto-Aim, Combo Starter |
| **Mortal Flamethrower** | 40 | 12s | **Guardbreak**, Ranged, Knockback |
| **Darkness Flame Sword** | 20 | 20s | **Guardbreak**, Upfling, Combo Finisher |
| **Darkness Flame Dragon** | High | N/A | Massive AOE, Reduces Self HP to 75% |

### Strategic Use of Awakening

The Jagan Eye transformation changes Hiei's flames from the weaker flames of the Human World to the devastating flames of the Demon World. **Mortal Flame Fist** is particularly powerful because it features auto-aim and teleports Hiei directly to the opponent, making it a perfect combo starter.

The **Darkness Flame Dragon** (correctly titled *Dragon of the Darkness Flame*) is Hiei's ultimate trump card. It seeks out the nearest target and deals massive damage. However, the cost is high—using it will automatically drop your health to 75%. Use this only when you are certain it will secure a kill or if you are already low on health.

<Callout type="warning" title="High Risk Warning">
Using **Darkness Flame Dragon** when you are at full health is generally discouraged unless you are finishing the match. The self-damage can put you at a disadvantage if the opponent survives.
</Callout>

</Section>
