<Section icon={Target} title="Core Move Structure & Control Schemes">

Every character in the game operates on a unified control scheme. This ensures that even when you switch between different **anime battle arena characters**, your muscle memory for movement, defense, and base attacks remains intact.

### Standard Control Layout

| Input | Action | Tactical Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Shift** | Sprint | Control spacing, close distances, or back away from pressure |
| **Mouse1 (M1)** | Basic Attack | Initiate pressure, build hit-stun, and start core combos |
| **Space + Mouse1** | Aerial Attack | Launch opponents upward to perform extended air combos |
| **F** | Block | Mitigate incoming frontal damage and predict heavy attacks |
| **Q** | Dodge | Fast directional dash to escape pressure or reposition |
| **1, 2, 3, 4** | Special Moves | Character-specific skills used to extend combos or break guards |
| **G** | Transformation | Activate character awakening for enhanced stats and new moves |

<Callout type="info" title="Defense Note">
Never spam your dodge (Q) randomly. A wasted dodge leaves you incredibly vulnerable to long-lasting M1 strings, as opponents can easily track your landing and initiate a full-damage combo.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide for Beginners">

Executing consistent combos is the difference between winning and losing in competitive matches. Follow this step-by-step framework to practice and execute your first high-damage strings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Confirm the M1 Starter">
    Sprint toward your opponent using **Shift** and land a clean **Mouse1** basic attack. Do not throw out special moves first; use basic attacks to lock your opponent in place with hit-stun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute the Aerial Launcher">
    After landing 3 consecutive grounded M1 attacks, press **Space + Mouse1** to launch your opponent into the air. This begins an aerial ladder that prevents them from immediately blocking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chain Your Special Moves">
    While your opponent is suspended in mid-air, press keys **1, 2, 3, or 4** to trigger a fast, character-specific special move. This extends the combo duration and maximizes your damage output before they hit the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare the Awakening Finish">
    If your awakening bar is full, knock your opponent back, press **G** to transform, and immediately follow up with enhanced versions of your special skills to secure the knockout.
  </Step>
</Steps>
