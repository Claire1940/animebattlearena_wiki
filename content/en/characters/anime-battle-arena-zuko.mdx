<Section icon={Target} title="The Ultimate Zuko Combo Guide">

To succeed in high-tier PvP, you must move beyond basic M1 clicking. Zuko's kit is designed for "ladders"—combos that take the opponent into the air where they cannot easily escape.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initiation">
    Approach the opponent using **Shift-sprint**. Land 3 basic **M1 attacks**. Do not finish the 4th hit, as it will knock them away.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Guard Break">
    If the opponent is blocking, immediately use **Fire Kick (2)**. This will stun them and open them up for the next phase of the combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Transition">
    Press **Space + M1** to launch the opponent into the air. While airborne, land two more M1s to maintain the height.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Skill Integration">
    While still in the air, use **Fire Blast (1)**. The burn effect will apply immediately. Follow this up with **Fire Whip (3)** to slam them back to the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Finisher">
    As they hit the ground, use **Explosive Blast (4)**. If timed correctly, the explosion will hit just as their invincibility frames end.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Mastery">
Practicing this sequence in a private server is highly recommended. The timing between Step 3 and Step 4 is the most common point where players drop the combo.
</Callout>

</Section>
