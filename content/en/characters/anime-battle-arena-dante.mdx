<Section icon={Swords} title="Detailed Moveset Analysis">

Understanding the nuances of Dante's kit is essential for climbing the ranked ladder. Each move serves a specific purpose, whether it is for initiating a fight, extending a combo, or finishing off a low-health target. In 2026, the frame data for Dante has been finely tuned to reward aggressive playstyles.

| Move Name | Input | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stinger** | Z | Gap Closer | Fast forward thrust that breaks guards/stuns. |
| **Million Stab** | X | Combo Extender | Rapid thrusts dealing multi-hit damage. |
| **Prop Shredder** | C | Launcher | Upward spin that sends enemies into the air. |

Dante's **Stinger** is perhaps his most iconic move. It allows you to close the distance instantly, catching opponents off-guard. When followed up with **Prop Shredder**, you can transition into an aerial combo that most characters struggle to escape without using a dedicated evasive maneuver.

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Note">
Ebony & Ivory (V) should be used sparingly. While great for stopping an opponent's health regeneration or keeping them at bay, it leaves you stationary for a brief moment, making you vulnerable to long-range projectiles.
</Callout>

**Strategic Use of Moves:**
- **Z (Stinger)**: Best used immediately after a successful dash or as a follow-up to a blocked M1 string.
- **X (Million Stab)**: Use this when the opponent is cornered or stuck in hit-stun to maximize the number of hits.
- **C (Prop Shredder)**: The primary tool for starting "air-to-ground" loops.

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Combo Guide">

To truly master **anime battle arena dante**, you must learn how to link his basic attacks with his specials. Combos are the bread and butter of ABA gameplay, and Dante has some of the most creative routes in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initiation">
    Land 3-4 M1 (basic) attacks. On the final hit, instead of the knockback, cancel into **Z (Stinger)** to keep the opponent close.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Launcher">
    Immediately follow the Stinger with **C (Prop Shredder)**. This will launch the opponent into the air. Jump immediately after the move connects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Pressure">
    Perform an aerial M1 string (usually 3 hits). While in the air, use your **X (Million Stab)** to keep them suspended and deal multi-hit damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    As you both fall back to the ground, use **V (Ebony & Ivory)** for a few quick shots or wait to land and reset for another M1 string if their guard is still broken.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| **Basic M1 String** | Easy | Low-Medium |
| **Stinger Loop** | Medium | High |
| **Full Air Combo** | Hard | Very High |
| **DT Burst Combo** | Medium | Extreme |
