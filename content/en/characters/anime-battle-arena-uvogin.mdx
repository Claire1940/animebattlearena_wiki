<Section icon={Map} title="Base Moveset & Kinetic Breakdown">

Uvogin's base kit is designed to keep opponents guessing while maintaining constant offensive pressure. By utilizing his Nen-enhanced physical strikes, he can easily transition from defensive play to explosive offensive sequences.

### Base Skills Overview

- **Scream**: Uvogin lets out a massive roar that crumples nearby enemies. This move ignores guard entirely. If you land a basic attack (M1) on a crumpled enemy, the damage is increased, though it will knock them back.
- **Rock Throw**: A fast-moving projectile where Uvogin hurls a large rock straight ahead. It deals high block damage, making it a safe tool to chip away at defensive opponents.
- **Ren**: Uvogin flares his aura, boosting his basic attack damage by 20% and multiplying his skill damage by 1.3x. While active, this skill is replaced by **Slam**.
  - **Slam**: A command grab where Uvogin grabs the opponent by the leg and slams them repeatedly into the dirt. It is blockable but serves as an excellent combo extender.
- **Impact**: A forward-traveling rush punch that acts as a guard break. If used on an opponent crumpled by Scream, the damage increases significantly.

| Skill Name | Damage (Standard) | Damage (Scream Active) | Damage (Ren Active) | Cooldown | Properties |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Scream** | 10 | 11 | 13 | 15s | Ignores Guard, Crumples |
| **Rock Throw** | 14 | 15.4 | 18.2 | 12s | Blockable, High Block DMG |
| **Slam (Ren)** | 22 | N/A | N/A | 14s | Blockable, Grab, Extender |
| **Impact** | 20 | 22 | 26 | 16s | Guardbreak, Knockback |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">

To maximize your efficiency with **anime battle arena uvogin**, you must learn how to seamlessly transition between his base kit attacks and his awakened skills.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Ren Buffer Setup">
    Start your engagement by activating **Ren** early during your basic attack strings. This ensures all subsequent hits receive the 1.3x damage multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Crumple Transition">
    Land 5 basic M1 attacks, then immediately execute **Scream** to crumple the opponent. While they are kneeling, execute the **Slam** grab to reset their positioning.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Impact Finisher">
    Follow up the Slam with another 5 M1s, and end the sequence with **Impact** to deal massive knockback damage and send the opponent flying.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Awakened Burst Loop">
    In awakened form, execute 4 M1s, launch **Boulder Throw**, follow up with 4 M1s, transition into **Enhanced Speed** (which guarantees a downslam if buffered correctly), and finish with **Smokescreen** for stealth pressure.
  </Step>
