- **Kaioken**: A temporary self-buff that increases speed and damage output but drains health over time. Managing this state is key to winning neutral exchanges.
- **Dragon Combo**: A rapid strike sequence that launches the opponent. This is your primary combo tool and can be linked with other attacks.
- **Spirit Bomb**: A slow-moving, high-damage projectile. It has a large hitbox and can break guards if timed correctly, making it excellent for pressure.
