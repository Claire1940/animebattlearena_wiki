<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Restraint Form: Awakening Guide">

When Alucard releases his Level One limiter, he enters the **Restraint Form**. This transformation shifts his playstyle from a gunslinger to a brutal melee combatant. This form is particularly effective against defensive players who hide behind blocks, as several of his moves either ignore or shatter guards entirely.

  <Step num="1" title="Vampiric Strength">

  <Step num="2" title="Raging Assault">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spear Hand">
    A large swipe that changes properties in the air. The air variant allows Alucard to float briefly before slamming toward the cursor. Killing an enemy with the air slam significantly reduces the move's cooldown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Spear Hand Tech">

<Section icon={Target} title="Count Form: The Ultimate Awakening">

The **Count Form** represents Alucard at Level Zero. In this state, he gains access to his sword and blood-based sorcery. This form provides the highest damage potential in his kit but requires careful management of the "River of Crimson" charges.

| Move Name | Damage | Property | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Impaler** | 25 | Ignores Guard | Dashes forward with a sword to impale and crumple the enemy. |
| **Giant's Slash** | 30 | Guardbreak | A massive horizontal sword swing with significant range. |
| **River of Crimson** | 20 | Ignores Guard | Creates a blood wave that steals health; has 3 charges. |
| **Card Throw** | 18 / 25 | Blockable | Summons Tubalcain/Rip Van Winkle for projectile support. |

<Callout type="info" title="River of Crimson Management">
**River of Crimson** creates a blood trail that waves every 5 seconds. Since it has 3 charges and regains one every 20 seconds, you should space out your usage to maintain constant area pressure and healing.
</Callout>
