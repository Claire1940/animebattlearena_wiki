### Base Move Mechanics

- **Banishing Salt Punch**: A lightning-fast, close-range punch coated in table salt. It acts as an instant guardbreak if your opponent is holding down block.
- **Salt Splash**: Reigen dashes forward and throws table salt to blind the enemy. When used during a standard M1 string, Reigen skips the dash animation entirely to spray salt instantly, making it a premier combo extender.
- **Facial Expressions**: A purely defensive panic button. It grants brief invincibility frames (I-frames) to dodge incoming ultimate attacks while generating 1.5 bars of your awakening meter.
- **Anti-Esper Dropkick**: Reigen launches himself forward with a dropkick. While it guardbreaks and forces a downsmash, it suffers from terrible end lag if blocked or dodged, making it highly punishable in high-tier ranked play.

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Combos & Extensions">

To succeed with Reigen in ranked PvP, you must master his combo extensions. Reigen cannot rely on raw strength in his base form; instead, he must use basic M1 strings and downslams to set up his unblockable blinding moves.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Begin with a standard 4-hit M1 ground string. Do not perform the final knockback hit; instead, execute an uptilt to launch the opponent into the air.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Aerial Follow-up">
    Jump and land 3 aerial M1 hits, then execute a clean downslam to send the opponent back to the arena floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hypnosis into Salt Splash">
    Immediately upon landing, activate your **Hypnosis Punch** (or direct M1 buffering) and cancel it directly into **Salt Splash**. This blinds the opponent and prevents them from blocking your next attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    While the opponent is blinded, execute another quick M1 string and finish with **Banishing Salt Punch** to break any panic blocks and secure the knockback.
  </Step>
</Steps>
