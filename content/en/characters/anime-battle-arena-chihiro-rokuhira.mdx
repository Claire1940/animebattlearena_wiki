Chihiro's kit is designed to flow. You typically start with **Nishiki** to increase your engagement speed, use **Kuro** to force a block or a dodge, and then capitalize with M1s. If the opponent tries to counter-attack with a projectile, **Aka** provides the perfect transition back into your offensive pressure.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide">

To maximize your damage with **anime battle arena chihiro rokuhira**, you must master the "Ladder" technique. ABA's physics allow sword users like Chihiro to keep opponents airborne for extended periods if the inputs are timed correctly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Approach the opponent using Shift-sprint. Land 3 basic M1 hits. Do not finish the 4th hit (which causes knockback) unless you are near a wall.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Uptilt Launch">
    After the 3rd M1, press **Space + Mouse1** simultaneously. This launches the opponent into the air, beginning the aerial portion of the combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Extension">
    While in the air, land 2 more M1s. Immediately follow up with **Skill 1 (Kuro)**. The ink wave will keep the opponent suspended in the hit-stun animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Nishiki Reset">
    If **Skill 3 (Nishiki)** is active, your fall speed is reduced. Land on the ground before the opponent and prepare for a second M1 string as they descend.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Finisher">
    Finish the sequence with **Skill 4 (Kuro: Shred)**. This will deal massive damage and send the opponent flying, giving you time to reset your cooldowns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

In the competitive 1v1 ladder, **anime battle arena chihiro rokuhira** is considered a high-tier threat. Success in ranked requires knowing when to play patiently and when to go all-in with your transformation.

| Opponent Tier | Difficulty | Strategy for Chihiro |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S-Tier (Gojo/Broly)** | Hard | Bait their AOE moves, use Aka to absorb, then punish. |
| **A-Tier (Ichigo/Aizen)** | Skill-Based | Focus on M1 priority; Nishiki is your best friend here. |

However, the duration is shorter than many other characters, meaning you must secure a kill within the first 30 seconds of activation.
