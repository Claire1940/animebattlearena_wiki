### Base Moveset Analysis

Afro's base moveset is designed to punish aggressive opponents while keeping his own combo pressure alive. He possesses a unique mix of guard-breaking options and defensive frames that can turn the tide of a match in an instant.

| Move | Damage | Cooldown | Properties | Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Counter Cut** | 25 | 19s (8s if missed) | Bypasses Guard, I-frames, Knock-back | 1 Stud |
| **Jump** | 15 | 10s | Blockable, Combo Extender | 5 Studs |
| **Phantom Cut** | 20 | 15s | Guard-break, Knock-back, Finisher | 3 Studs |
| **Triple Strike** | 36 | 15s | Guard-break (last hit), Combo Starter | 3.25 Studs |

*   **Counter Cut**: Afro Samurai holds his blade ready. If struck, he slashes back, dealing 25 damage with full invincibility frames (I-frames). If no one strikes, he sheathes his sword, triggering a reduced 8-second cooldown.
*   **Jump**: A highly versatile mobility tool. If used near an opponent, Afro leaps off their head, kicking them for 15 damage and resetting his air state for further combo extensions.
*   **Phantom Cut**: Afro turns translucent and travels forward. Upon reappearing, he unleashes a massive slash. This move is famous for its generous hitbox and guard-breaking properties.
*   **Triple Strike**: Afro turns translucent and slashes three times. It serves as an excellent combo starter or a panic option. Because of its tiny startup animation, you can use it to strike back mid-pressure.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Damage Trade">
If you anticipate getting hit by a slow, high-damage move (such as Broly's Eraser Blow), execute **Triple Strike** immediately. You will trade damage, break their momentum, and often come out ahead in total health.
</Callout>

### Key Strategies for Competitive Play

*   **Teammate Counter Baiting**: In 2v2 or team game modes, Afro's Counter Cut can actually be triggered by your own teammates' attacks. If a teammate strikes your counter-stance while an enemy is nearby, the counter will trigger instantly, catching the opponent off guard.
*   **Counter Canceling**: Be aware that Counter Cut can be canceled if an opponent hits you before the startup animation fully begins. Do not throw it out predictably; instead, use it right as the opponent commits to an M1 string.
*   **Spacing with Phantom Cut**: Phantom Cut has an exceptionally wide horizontal hitbox. Use this to catch opponents who attempt to dash sideways or circle-strafe around you.
