### Detailed Move Breakdown

- **Jaw Kick**: Akaza strikes upward, launching the enemy slightly without a heavy knockback. Because it breaks guards and leaves the opponent close, it is your absolute best tool for starting or extending a grounded M1 sequence.
- **Compass Needle**: Akaza deploys his signature snowflake compass under his feet. This move grants temporary invincibility frames (I-frames) upon startup, boosts your movement speed, increases your M1 damage, and allows you to track opponents through walls.
- **First Smash**: A leaping strike where Akaza slams his fist directly into the floor. It creates a shockwave that launches nearby enemies into the air, making it an excellent follow-up after a downslam.
- **Disorder**: A rapid-fire barrage of shockwave punches. While it can be blocked initially, it deals heavy chip damage and serves as a reliable combo filler when your opponent's dodge is on cooldown.

<Callout type="warning" title="Compass Needle Startup">
Compass Needle has a 3-second startup animation. While you do receive I-frames during this time, do not activate it when your opponent is actively rushing you down with fast, unblockable skills.
</Callout>
