<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Form Moveset Analysis">

Metal Bat's base moveset is designed to punish defensive players while offering excellent combo-starting and extending capabilities. Understanding the properties of each swing is critical for securing wins in both ranked and casual matches.

| Move Name | Damage | Properties | Cooldown | Combat Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Relentless Strikes** | 22 (4 x 4 + 6) | Blockable, Guardbreak (last hit) | Moderate | Combo Starter & Extender |
| **Brutal Tornado** | 25 (1.8 x 10 + 7) | Blockable | Moderate | Multi-hit Combo Finisher |
| **Fighting Spirit** | Counter (Enrage) | Ignores Guard, I-Frames | Long | Defensive Counter & DMG Buffer |
| **Home Run** | 26 to 31 | Guardbreak | Moderate | Aggressive Knockback Finisher |

### Detailed Move Breakdown

- **Relentless Strikes**: Metal Bat strikes the opponent five times in quick succession. The final hit slams the target into the ground, breaking their guard if they try to block the entire sequence. This is your primary tool for extending basic attack chains.
- **Brutal Tornado**: A spinning vortex of strikes that pulls nearby enemies into his orbit before kicking them away. It is highly effective for catching opponents who attempt to dodge sideways.
- **Fighting Spirit**: This is Metal Bat's counter move. Upon activation, he steadies himself. If hit during this window, he clinches the attacker, becomes enraged, and gains a temporary damage boost ranging from **14% to 21%** depending on his current state.
- **Home Run**: A heavily charged, high-impact swing that instantly breaks guards. The damage scales up based on his Fighting Spirit level:
  - **Base Damage**: 26
  - **Fighting Spirit v1**: 29.5
  - **Fighting Spirit v2**: 31

<Callout type="warning" title="Counter Advisory">
Fighting Spirit has a distinct startup animation. Experienced players will stop attacking if they see you steadying yourself, leaving you vulnerable to grabs or delayed guardbreaks. Use it unpredictably.
</Callout>

### Key Awakening Mechanics

- **Brutal Hurricane**: An enhanced version of Brutal Tornado. If the final strike does not connect with an opponent, Metal Bat casts the spinning tornado forward as a projectile, pulling distant enemies toward him.
- **Recovery Bash**: One of Metal Bat's most versatile tools. It functions as an active attack that slams enemies to the ground, but it can also be used as an **evasive recovery** while you are being comboed.
- **Ultimate**: A devastating single-target grab that deals a flat **500 damage**, securing an instant kill on any caught opponent. This can only be used once per life.
