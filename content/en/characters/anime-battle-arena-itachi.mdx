<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Moveset & Tactical Utility">

Itachi's base kit allows him to control the pace of the match by punishing mistakes and maintaining optimal distance. Understanding the properties of each move is essential to maximizing his combat efficiency.

### Crow Illusion & Exploding Clone
This is Itachi's signature defensive tool. Upon activation, Itachi creates a hand sign and controls a crow clone for 5 seconds. If the clone takes damage or expires, Itachi reverts to his original position with his health restored to the exact amount he had when activating the move.
- **Double Tap Variant (Exploding Clone):** Pressing the key again sends out an aggressive clone that deals 12 damage and breaks the opponent's guard.

### Amaterasu (Base)
Itachi waves his hand to release black flames that track the opponent until they make contact or hit a solid wall.
- **Base Damage:** 26 total (20 initial + 6 damage over time).
- **Shuriken Assault (Air Variant):** Activating this move in mid-air launches a rapid barrage of shurikens instead of the tracking flame.

### Fireball Jutsu & Phoenix Flower
A versatile projectile that shoots directly toward your cursor.
- **Properties:** Breaks guard and deals 20 damage.
- **Phoenix Flower Variant:** Pressing the input immediately after Crow Illusion fires a wider, multi-hit barrage of smaller fireballs.

### Demonic Illusion
Itachi traps his target in a genjutsu, impaling them with giant spikes and completely immobilizing them. This acts as his primary combo starter or extender.

### The Susanoo Shield & Yata Mirror
Summoning the Susanoo ribcage provides a passive visual effect, but holding the block key (F) activates its true potential:
- **Yata Mirror Push:** While holding F, Itachi blocks all incoming attacks. 
- **Totsuka Blade Counter:** Once the shield absorbs enough damage, Itachi automatically charges forward and slams the opponent with the Totsuka Blade, dealing between 10 to 55 damage.
- **Double Tap Variant:** Pressing the Susanoo key twice instantly triggers a manual downslam dealing 40 damage.

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Strategy">
You can perform basic attacks (M1s) and cast other spells like Tsukuyomi or Amaterasu while the Susanoo shield is actively protecting you. Use this to safely pressure opponents.
</Callout>
