<Section icon={Target} title="Essential Combos and Controls">

Mastering the movement and attack inputs is the difference between a novice and a pro in ABA. Rimuru's combos rely heavily on the "ladder" mechanic—launching an opponent into the air and following them up for additional hits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Approach the opponent using **Shift** to sprint. Land 3 standard **Mouse1** hits. Do not finish the 4th hit on the ground if you want to extend the combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Uptilt Launch">
    After the 3rd M1, press **Space + Mouse1** simultaneously. This will launch the opponent into the air, allowing you to jump and continue the chain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aerial Extension">
    While in the air, land 2-3 more M1 hits. This is known as "laddering." It keeps the opponent in hit-stun and prevents them from using most escape moves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Skill Integration">
    As you begin to fall, activate **Water Blade (1)** or **Black Flame (2)**. The projectile will hit the opponent just as they are about to regain control.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Finisher">
    If the opponent is near a wall, use **Beelzebub (3)** to slam them into the environment, resetting your cooldowns and granting a small heal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Stamina">
Over-extending combos can leave you vulnerable. If you see your opponent's "Substitution" or "Evasive" cooldown is up, be prepared to use **Q** to dodge away immediately after your finisher.
</Callout>

| **Ladder** | 3x M1 + Uptilt + 3x Air M1 + Black Flame | Medium | High |
