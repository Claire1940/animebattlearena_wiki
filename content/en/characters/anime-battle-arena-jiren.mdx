### Deep Dive into Base Skills

Jiren's base kit is highly versatile, offering tools for every situation:

- **Begone**: Fires a red-orange ki blast dragging enemies along. At close range, Jiren grabs the enemy and forces the blast directly into them. The close-range variant is unblockable.
- **Glare**: Jiren pummels the space in front of him with invisible ki blows. This move grants active invincibility frames (Iframes) and can redirect enemy beam attacks.
- **Grand Charge / Spiraling Justice**: A forward charge that launches enemies. In the air, this transforms into Spiraling Justice, where Jiren spins and kicks the opponent back to the ground.
- **Power Counter**: Jiren coats his hand in red ki. If struck during this state, he immediately retaliates with a high-damage blast that breaks guards.

<Callout type="warning" title="Cooldown Alert">
Using Begone at close range increases its cooldown from 13 seconds to 23 seconds. Only use the grab version if you are guaranteed to secure a knock-out or need to bypass an opponent's tight guard.
</Callout>

### Matchup Strategies

- **Against Zone Characters**: Do not chase them blindly. Stand your ground and meditate. This forces them to stop running and approach you, bringing them into range of your Grand Charge.
- **Against Aggressive Rushdowns**: Keep your Power Counter ready. Predictable dash attacks are easily parried by Jiren's counter, turning their offense into a 30-damage punish for you.

<Callout type="warning" title="Positioning Rules">
Never activate Power Counter while far away from the enemy. Smart players will wait out the active frames and punish your recovery lag with a full combo.
</Callout>
