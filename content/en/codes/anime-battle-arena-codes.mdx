<Section icon={Target} title="How to Execute Advanced Combos">

To move beyond the beginner level, you must learn how to chain your basic attacks and special skills into a seamless loop. This process is often referred to as "Combo-ing." The most effective strategy is to use your Mouse1 (M1) string to stun the opponent before using a skill that keeps them in hit-stun.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Approach the opponent and land 3 to 4 consecutive Mouse1 hits. This creates enough hit-stun to prevent them from blocking your next move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Aerial Transition">
    After the third M1, press **Space + Mouse1** to launch the opponent into the air. This is the start of an "Air Combo" or "Ladder."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Skill Integration">
    While the opponent is mid-air or just as they land, activate one of your special moves (Keys 1-4). Choose a move that has a fast startup time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Awakening Closer">
    If your Awakening meter is full, press **G** to transform. Most transformations reset your cooldowns, allowing you to repeat the combo for a finishing blow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Training Mode">
Use a private server or the training area to practice these timings. Landing an aerial attack consistently is the difference between a Gold and Diamond rank player.
</Callout>
